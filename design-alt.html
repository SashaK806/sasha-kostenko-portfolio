Alt: Queer
Russian Design

Print Design, Motion Graphics

In deciding who to represent in an informational poster about design history, I considered the history of the Russian LGBTQ+ community in a nation that hasn't always accepted their presence. Work produced by or about the community speaks to their lives and their voices despite and in response to a turbulent socio-political history that punishes them for visibility.

The motion poster speaks to this tension, the letters needed to complete the title appearing and disappearing. The title itself — "goluboy" — references Russian slang for the community, also meaning "light blue".
