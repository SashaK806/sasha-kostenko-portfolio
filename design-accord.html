Accord

Interaction Design & Branding


Studies show that people often hold misconceptions about what will make them happier. Achieving goals may help us feel more fulfilled, but other factors like nourishing our social connections, healthy lifestyle habits, and mindful living have a greater impact on our happiness overall.

Accord is a tool that emphasizes these parts of our lives, without sacrificing working towards our goals. Instead, it emphasizes a balance between work and life, suggesting habits that improve well–being while supporting our work–related goals.
