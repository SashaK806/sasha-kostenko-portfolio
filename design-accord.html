Accord

Interaction Design & Branding


Nourishing social connections, practicing healthy lifestyle habits, and engaging in mindful living can have a profound impact on people's happiness. Accord is a tool that encourages users to invest in these aspects of their lives without sacrificing working towards their goals. Instead, it emphasizes a balance between work and life, suggesting habits that improve well–being while supporting work–related goals.

In contrast to other wellness and productivity tools, Accord's design considers users' lives holistically. It suggests activities for a happier life — such as reaching out to an old friend — alongside to–dos like focused work time. Users may further personalize their recommendations, adjusting them to fit their particular work–life balance. Accord's visual design reflects this holistic vision. The planetary visual theme of the app reflects each user's internal world, each person orbited by different, yet significant, aspects of their lives.
