An opening title sequence adapted from author Long Litt Woon's memoir "Into the Woods". After losing her husband, the main character slowly recovers from her mourning when she falls in love with foraging for mushrooms, finding a community of like–minded people and reconnecting with the natural world.

The sequence's imagery draws from the visual vocabulary of field notes and taxonomy, and was produced in Adobe After Effects and Photoshop.
