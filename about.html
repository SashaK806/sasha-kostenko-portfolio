About Me

sasha.kostenko@wustl.edu

Hello! I'm Sasha, a designer, illustrator, and creative coder studying Communication Design at Washington University in St. Louis. I grew up outside of Raleigh, NC, and currently live in St Louis, MO.

I'm passionate about storytelling and centering user experiences in my work. Some of my inspirations are adaptive design systems, motion graphics, game design, and narrative illustration. I'm always learning something new, from creative coding techniques to dabbling in another language. Ask me about my collection of unfinished crochet projects!

I currently do branding and graphic design work for Bloodhound Branding, and UX/UI design at the Health Communication Design Studio at WashU.
