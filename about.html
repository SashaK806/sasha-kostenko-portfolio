Sasha is a B.F.A. candidate at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis. She is majoring in Communication Design, concentrating in interaction and illustration, and minoring in Computer Science. She loves working across mediums, exploring how she can integrate illustration, coding, and animation, and incorporate them in her design work. Sasha is passionate about equitable and human-centered design, and collaborates with community partners to produce impactful work.

In her free time, she writes, gets outside, and watches the birds. She wants to fill a sketchbook with pigeons.
