Hello! I'm Sasha, a multi–disciplinary designer whose work spans product, UX/UI, motion, and graphic design. I'm passionate about centering human experiences in my work and believe in the power of design and creativity to improve lives and the world around us.

I grew up outside of Raleigh, NC, and currently live in St Louis, MO, where I received my B.F.A. in Communication Design at Washington University in St. Louis. I currently work as a Digital Producer at StoryTrack Studio.
