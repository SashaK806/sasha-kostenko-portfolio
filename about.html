About Me

sasha.kostenko@wustl.edu

Hello! I'm Sasha, a B.F.A. candidate at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis. I'm majoring in Communication Design, concentrating in interaction and illustration, and minoring in Computer Science. I love working across mediums, exploring how I can integrate illustration, coding, and animation, and incorporating them in my design work. I'm also passionate about equitable and human-centered design, and collaborate with community partners to produce impactful work.

In my free time, I write, get outside, and watch the birds. I want to fill a sketchbook with pigeons.
