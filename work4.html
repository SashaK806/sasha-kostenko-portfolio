This project adapted an existing Wikipedia article into a multi-page site. I worked from a peer's creative direction and choice of content to produce a site that spoke to their desired aesthetic and function. Ultimately, we chose a design that evoked nostalgia for the period in which this film came out. The "tv static" effect and play button navigation bar reference older video technology, and the color scheme pairs with the festive but muted palette of the film.
