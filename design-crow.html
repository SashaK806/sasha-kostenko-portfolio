How to Befriend
a Crow

2D Animation, Motion Graphics

A step-by-step guide on how to befriend your neighborhood crows, and the benefits of doing so. I developed a style that maintained the macabre tone traditionally associated with crows, but allowed for a looser, friendlier shape and movement in my animation. Some of my visual inspirations included sharpie on newspaper and collage.

Portions of the video were animated in Photoshop, then combined with other assets in Adobe After Effects.
